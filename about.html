About This App

Doctor Appointment App

Version 1.0

This application is designed to simplify the process of booking and managing appointments with your doctor. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform that helps you stay organized and connected with your healthcare provider.

Key Features:

  • Easy appointment scheduling
  • Access to your appointment history
  • Timely appointment reminders
  • View your prescriptions online
  • Manage your profile information
